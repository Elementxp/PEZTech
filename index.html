About us

PEZTech is a non-profit organization aiming to bring together tech enthusiasts and provide learning and networking opportunities. Founded by Peyton Gruber, Elliot Beaulieu, and Zacharie Morin. PEZTech is designed to help teens kickstart their journey in technology careers such as coding, IT, robotics, and more!
