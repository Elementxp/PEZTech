About us!

PEZTech is a non-profit organization aiming to bring together tech enthusiasts and provide learning and networking opportunities. Made by Peyton Gruber, Elliot Beaulieu, and Zacharie Morin. PEZTech was made for teens to kickstart their journey in technology careers like: Coding, IT, robotics, and more!
